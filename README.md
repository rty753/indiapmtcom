# IndiaPMT - Emerging Market Payment Gateway Solutions

> Professional third-party payment integration platform covering 17+ countries across South Asia, Southeast Asia, Latin America, Middle East & Africa.

**Live Site:** [https://indiapmt.com](https://indiapmt.com)

## Overview

IndiaPMT (印度支付) is a one-stop payment service platform that provides cross-border payment channel integration for businesses operating in emerging markets. We specialize in local payment method aggregation, game payment channels, and third-party payment system integration.

### Key Capabilities

- **17-Country Coverage** - Local payment channels for India, Indonesia, Vietnam, Thailand, Philippines, Pakistan, Bangladesh, Brazil, Mexico, Turkey, Iran, Egypt, Nigeria, South Africa, Kenya, Ethiopia, Congo
- **Local Payment Methods** - UPI, GCash, OVO, DANA, PIX, M-Pesa, bKash, JazzCash, PromptPay, and 50+ local wallets & bank transfers
- **Game Payment Channels** - Optimized payment flows for online gaming, iGaming, and digital entertainment
- **Third-Party Payment Integration** - Unified API for multi-country payment collection and payout
